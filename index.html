Rabbit R1 Remote
Tip: Use QR Scan tab to scan a QR code containing your token.
These open your HA UI directly (dashboards/views). If the R1 keeps you logged in, great — otherwise you may need to log in once.
Examples:
/lovelace
/lovelace/0
/dashboard-mushroom
Scan a QR code that contains either:
YOUR_LONG_LIVED_TOKEN
or JSON like:
{"token":"YOUR_TOKEN"}
